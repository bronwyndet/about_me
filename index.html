Introduction

Bronwyn Detlofsen is a front end developer and programmer as well as a small business entreuprenuer. She is the owner and manager of the geek-themed coffeehouse, Wayward Coffeehouse, located in north Seattle. After building her own business for the past 11 years, she is now eager to bring both her programming skills and business experience to a full-time position as a web developer/software engineer.

Bronwyn's professional career includes experience in marketing, entreuprenuership, travel and tourism, restaurant operations, and management.

As an avid traveller and having lived abroad early in her career, Bronwyn brings a broad range of personal experience and perspective to any position. As a parent, a motorcycle rider, and white water rafting enthusiast, Bronwyn knows how to take risks as well as how to mitigate those risks through preparation, experience, skill, and sometimes just a good sense of humor!
